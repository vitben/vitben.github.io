About Me

Born in Milan (Italy), where I studied Nuclear engineering, in 2014 I moved to Geneva (Switzerland) to work for CERN, the Nuclear Organization for Nuclear Research. Today I work for the AWAKE experiment at CERN, which aims to demonstrate principle of proton driven plasma wakefield acceleration. In my role, I develop leverage numerical optimization techqniques, machine learning algortithms and all sort of quantitavie methods with the purpose of imporving the performance of the experiment.
